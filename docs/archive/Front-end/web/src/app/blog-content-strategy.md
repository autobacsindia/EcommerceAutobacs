## Content Optimization Guidelines

### Keyword Targeting
- Primary keywords: "automotive accessories India", "car accessories online India", "bike accessories India"
- Secondary keywords: "[brand] accessories", "[vehicle model] accessories", "[product type] India"

### On-Page SEO
- Title tags: 50-60 characters with primary keyword
- Meta descriptions: 155-160 characters with call-to-action
- H1 tags: Include primary keyword and brand name
- Image optimization: Descriptive alt text with keywords

### Content Structure
- Introduction with keyword-rich opening paragraph
- Section headers (H2, H3) with semantic keywords
- Bullet points and numbered lists for readability
- Internal links to relevant product categories
- Call-to-action buttons for product discovery

## Publishing Schedule

- Weekly: 1 category-specific guide
- Bi-weekly: 1 seasonal/technical guide
- Monthly: 1 localized vehicle-specific guide

## Performance Tracking

- Track rankings for target keywords
- Monitor organic traffic growth
- Analyze engagement metrics (time on page, bounce rate)
- Measure conversion rates from blog to product pages
